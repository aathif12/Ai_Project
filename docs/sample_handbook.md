# Sample University Document - Student Handbook

## Welcome to Our University

This handbook contains essential information for all students enrolled at our university. Please read it carefully and keep it for future reference.

---

## Academic Policies

### Attendance Requirements

1. **Minimum Attendance**: Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in all courses to be eligible to sit for final examinations.

2. **Attendance Tracking**: Attendance is recorded electronically at the start of each lecture. Students are responsible for ensuring their attendance is marked.

3. **Medical Leave**: In case of illness, students must submit a medical certificate within 7 days of returning to classes. Medical certificates must be from a registered medical practitioner.

4. **Special Circumstances**: Students facing exceptional circumstances should contact the Student Affairs Office within 48 hours.

### Course Registration

1. **Registration Period**: Course registration opens two weeks before the start of each semester.

2. **Registration Deadline**: The final deadline for course registration is the end of the first week of the semester.

3. **Late Registration**: Late registration (after the deadline) incurs a fee of $50 and requires approval from the department head.

4. **Course Modification**: Students may add or drop courses within the first two weeks of the semester without penalty.

5. **Maximum Credits**: Undergraduate students may register for a maximum of 21 credits per semester. Students with a GPA above 3.5 may request to register for up to 24 credits.

### Examination Policies

1. **Final Exams**: Final examinations are held at the end of each semester during a designated examination period.

2. **Eligibility**: Students must meet the 75% attendance requirement to be eligible for examinations.

3. **Exam Schedule**: The examination schedule is published four weeks before the examination period.

4. **Re-examination**: Students who fail an examination may apply for re-examination within one week of results publication. A fee of $25 applies.

5. **Grading Scale**:
   - A: 90-100%
   - B: 80-89%
   - C: 70-79%
   - D: 60-69%
   - F: Below 60%

## Hostel Rules and Regulations

### Accommodation

1. **Eligibility**: Hostel accommodation is available to full-time students. Priority is given to students from outside the city.

2. **Application**: Students must apply for hostel accommodation before the start of each academic year. Applications open on June 1st.

3. **Room Allocation**: Rooms are allocated based on seniority and availability. First-year students share rooms; senior students may request single rooms.

### Hostel Rules

1. **Visitor Policy**: 
   - Visitors are allowed in common areas only between 9:00 AM and 8:00 PM.
   - Overnight guests are strictly prohibited.
   - All visitors must register at the hostel reception.

2. **Quiet Hours**: Quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM on weekdays and 11:00 PM to 8:00 AM on weekends.

3. **Cleanliness**: Students are responsible for maintaining cleanliness in their rooms. Weekly room inspections are conducted.

4. **Prohibited Items**:
   - Cooking appliances (except electric kettles)
   - Pets
   - Alcohol and tobacco products
   - Loud musical instruments

5. **Curfew**: The hostel gate closes at 11:00 PM on weekdays and midnight on weekends.

## Student Services

### Library

1. **Operating Hours**:
   - Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
   - Saturday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
   - Sunday: Closed

2. **Borrowing**: Students may borrow up to 5 books at a time for a period of 14 days.

3. **Late Fees**: A fine of $1 per day applies for overdue books.

### Health Services

1. **Medical Center**: The campus medical center provides free consultations for enrolled students.

2. **Operating Hours**: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM.

3. **Emergency**: For after-hours emergencies, contact campus security at ext. 999.

### Career Services

1. **Career Counseling**: Free career counseling is available by appointment.

2. **Job Portal**: Students can access job listings through the university career portal.

3. **Resume Review**: The career center offers free resume review services.
